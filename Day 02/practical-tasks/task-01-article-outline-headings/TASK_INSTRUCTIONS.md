# Task 01 — Article Outline With Headings

## Summary

Build a short **reading-style article** (about 250–400 words total) on a topic you choose (course-related is ideal) using a correct heading outline: one `<h1>`, multiple `<h2>` sections, and at least one `<h3>` subsection.

## Learning goals

- Practice heading levels without skipping (`h1` → `h2` → `h3`).
- Wrap the article in `<article>` with optional `<header>` for title/byline.

## Provided files

- `index.html`, `styles/main.css`

## What you will implement

1. Complete all `TODO(Day02-task01)` items.
2. Original prose (no lorem ipsum).

## How to run and verify

Open `index.html` in a browser; use headings map in Firefox “Headings” sidebar or similar.
