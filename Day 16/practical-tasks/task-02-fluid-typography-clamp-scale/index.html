Release notes

Stabilize the renderer

This update makes the renderer more stable and improves performance. It also gives smoother results on different screen sizes.

What changed

This update fixes small problems in the renderer. Pages load more smoothly and look more stable. The system does less extra work while drawing content. Text and images stay in the correct place. Scrolling feels smoother on most devices.The changes also help improve speed. Overall, the app is more reliable and easier to use.

Migration notes

You do not need to make many changes. Check how your layout looks on every viewport size. Use clamp() to keep text at a good size. A simple scale makes the design look better. Test your pages on small and large screens. Fix any spacing if needed. After that, everything should work well.
