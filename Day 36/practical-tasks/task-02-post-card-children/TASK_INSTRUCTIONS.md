# Task 2 — `PostCard` title + body (`children`)

## Summary

Model a simple **article card** with a fixed **title region** and a **body region** filled by `children`.

## Learning goals

- Combine explicit props (`title`) with `children` for rich content.

## What you will implement

1. `PostCard` renders `title` in `<h2 className="card__title">`.
2. `children` render inside `<section className="card__body">`.
